Walks

Welcome to the Galloway Farm Park walks section, we have multiple walks that you can choose to see the rich landscape.

Brook Walk

The Brooke walk is our shortest walk in which the you walk around a small lake which has benches where you can eat lunch.

Blackwood Walk

The Blackwood walk is the longest walk, this walk is recommended only for the vert fit. It involves walking all the way around the farm, north to south. This walk takes about 3 hours.

South Walk

This is the walk that circles around the area of the farm where the animals are kept near the petting zoo which you will be able to see from behind. It takes about an hour, the walk ends just by the cafe at the southend of the farm.

Walkpath concerns

We are repairing the walkpaths on august 17th-29th. During this time the walks will not be in service as there will be work being done on the paths.
