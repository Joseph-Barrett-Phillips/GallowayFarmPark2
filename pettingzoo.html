Petting Zoo

Welcome to the Galloway Farm Park Petting Zoo section, on our land we have a petting zoo with various different animals that the customer can feed and play with.

Lamas

At our petting zoo we have lamas, 9 of them, all are well behaved and can be fed without biting.

Horses

Our petting zoo also has horses, 6, which people can pet and feed and even ride. They have all recieved training to behave during the sessions.

Goats

We also have Goats at the Galloway Farm Park, they have also been trained to behave and not act aggressively to customers who want to feed them.
