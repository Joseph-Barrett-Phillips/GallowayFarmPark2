Home

Welcome to the Galloway Farm Park, we are an attraction that provides you with an amazing landscape that you can enjoy whilst you are here as well as great walks and great food.

History

The Galloway Farm Park is an old farm that has been transformed into a center for various activities. We originally came into possession of this land when our ancestor was victorious in a battle against his enemy and my ancestor got gifted this land as a token of thanks from the local lord.

Health & Safety

We take health and safety very serious here as we always make sure the walks are safe for walks and all the holes are filled to avoid falling. We also have safe animals too pet, they have been trained to be human friendly. Furthermore we have a lot of options for eaters who want to avoid specific food types for health reasons at the cafe

What you need

It is advised that you bring walking boots as whilst the walks are kept safe they are still hard on the soles of shoes. However we also sell walking boots at a modest price if you need any. Furthermore if you need assistance with walking we can give you a guide to help you with directions or with physically walking.

About Us

We are a company that sits between edinburgh and carlisle. We have a long tradition of looking about our animals and our lands going back 200 years. This is a tradition passed down through the family.
